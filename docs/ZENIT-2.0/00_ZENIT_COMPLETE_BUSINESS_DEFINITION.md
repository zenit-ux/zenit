# SECTION 3: CORE BUSINESS MODEL

## The Shift (What's Changing)

### BEFORE: Permanent Employment Model

**How it worked:**
- Companies hired permanent teams
- Teams worked at one company for years
- Large consulting firms (middlemen) connected them
- Middlemen took 25-35% cut
- Teams saw 65-75% of what companies paid

**Why it existed:**
- Companies needed stability (operations, culture)
- Teams needed security (salary, benefits)
- No alternatives existed
- Trust required intermediary with reputation

**Why it's breaking:**
- IA automates routine work (permanent staff unnecessary)
- Agents handle operations (flexibility beats headcount)
- Remote-first removes geography constraints
- Project-based work is now viable

### NOW: On-Demand Flexible Model

**How it works:**
- Companies contract teams per project (not permanent)
- Teams work on multiple projects per year
- Zenit connects both (fair platform, not extractive)
- Zenit takes 15% commission
- Teams see 85% of what companies pay

**Why this works:**
- Companies get flexibility (no permanent costs)
- Teams get variety (not stuck at one company)
- Both get economics benefit (no 30% middleman cut)
- Zenit scales without offices/overhead

**Why Zenit wins:**
- Lower commission (15% vs 30%)
- Faster matching (24-48 hours vs 2-3 weeks)
- Better quality (4.8★ vetted vs unvetted)
- Fair rates (marketplace sets them, not middleman)

## Three Core Pillars (Zenit's Value)

### Pillar 1: VALIDATES

**What we do:**
Verify every squad is production-ready. 4.8★ average.

**How we validate:**
- Code quality (review GitHub, assess code)
- Delivery track record (past projects, on-time history)
- Communication (test with mock project, assess responsiveness)
- Reliability (background check, ghosting history)
- Team stability (how long they stay together)

**For Standard projects:**
- Verify engineering competence
- Verify delivery capability
- Verify communication skills

**For AI projects:**
- Same as above PLUS
- AI project delivery history (proof of AI work)
- Domain expertise (fintech, healthtech, etc.)
- Mentorship capability (can teach, not just code)
- Architecture understanding (not just implementation)

**Result:** Only teams that have proven they can deliver are in Zenit.

### Pillar 2: MATCHES

**What we do:**
Find the 3-5 perfect fits. Fast. Accurate. Personalized.

**How we match:**
1. Kaizen AI interviews company (discovers actual need)
2. Kaizen AI analyzes company tech stack, culture, timeline
3. Matching algorithm searches 500+ vetted squads
4. Filters by: skill, experience, ZenitRank, cultural fit, availability
5. Returns top 3-5 matches
6. Company interviews 3-5 people (not 100)

**For Standard projects:**
- Match by: React, Node, Python, DevOps, etc.
- Match by: Project complexity (startup vs enterprise)
- Match by: Company culture (startup vs corporate)
- Match by: Timeline (2 weeks vs 6 months)

**For AI projects:**
- Same as above PLUS
- Match by: AI domain (computer vision, NLP, recommendations, etc.)
- Match by: Industry expertise (fintech = fraud detection, healthtech = diagnostics, etc.)
- Match by: Company's learning capability (can they absorb knowledge)
- Match by: Current team's tech level (mentorship level needed)

**Result:** Both company and squad choose best fit. Win-win, not compromise.

### Pillar 3: PROTECTS

**What we do:**
Guarantee outcome. Protect both sides. Fair guarantees.

**How we protect:**

**For Companies:**
- SafePay escrow: Money held until deliverables proven
- GitHub proof: All code visible in real-time (no black box)
- Squad B backup: If Team A fails mid-project, Team B takes over in 24h (no restart)
- Milestone releases: Payment released only when milestone complete and proven
- Legal protection: Simple contracts, clear terms

**For Squads:**
- Fair rates: Company can't negotiate down after matching
- Payment guarantee: SafePay ensures company can't refuse payment
- Reputation protection: ZenitRank shows pattern (one bad client doesn't destroy rep)
- No surprises: Clear scope, clear requirements, clear communication

**Result:** Both sides feel safe. Work gets done. Payment happens.

- Tax implications (commission vs salary, 1099 vs employment)
- LatAm: Regulatory landscape unclear

### Impact if it happens
- SafePay can't operate (need legal entity)
- Payment processing blocked (OFAC, sanctions, etc.)
- Tax liability (unclear status)
- Revenue interrupted

### Mitigation Strategies

**Strategy 1: Legal + compliance from start**
- Work with lawyer (specializing in LatAm, tech, payments)
- Understand regulations before launch
- Structure appropriately (holding company, jurisdiction, etc.)

**Strategy 2: Flexible payment options**
- Don't rely only on SafePay escrow
- Have alternative: Stripe Connect, bank transfer, etc.
- If one blocked, others still work

**Strategy 3: Tax clarity**
- Squads are contractors (1099-like), not employees
- Commission is service fee (not wage)
- Clear documentation for all parties
- Work with accountant on structure
